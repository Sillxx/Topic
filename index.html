J :Tonight , one of the most successful authors is with us , John Grisham ! So Mr.John , tell us how your career progressed.
JG : I started my career as a lawyer although I really liked baseball , I was devoting my little time for writing.
J : What's your first novel ? What inspired you to write it ?
JG : As I was working in a case , I heard the testimony of a little girl . That was how I got its idea and it took me three years to finish "A Time To Kill".
J : Was it easy to publish it ?
JG : No , not at all , I got rejected by many publishers.
J : They must have regretted it ! May I ask what's the secret of success in your opinion?
JG : Turning pages , you must have a story that makes the reader turn the pages . The plot to be intelligent and you need interesting characters , so that your readers care about them . What I'm trying to do is just write a good popular fiction.
J : And how is your life going on now ?
JG : My life has changed since then and I like spending quality-time with my kids .
J : Thanks Mr.John . That was it . Good-Bye
JG : Bye
